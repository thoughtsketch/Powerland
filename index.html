Experience the Allure of Bopdev Ghat

Perched at the southern edge of Pune, Bopdev Ghat is a stunning hill pass that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and accessibility. Situated on the Pune-Saswad Road, this serene destination lies just 10 km from bustling localities like Kondhwa and NIBM, making it a hidden gem for those seeking peace without straying far from city conveniences. For spiritual seekers, the region is rich in heritage, with iconic spots like the Kaas Plateau (Valley of Flowers) and the famous Khandoba Temple at Jejuri within driving distance. The nearby areas are dotted with farms and scenic trails, making it a haven for adventure enthusiasts, trekkers, and cyclists.
